Eight challenges, year-round

Each runs as a 3-round elimination over ~3 months. One entry fee covers all rounds.

How a challenge works

Same mechanic across all eight formats. Different word count, different genre rules.
1

Get your prompt

You're placed into a group of ~40 writers. Everyone in your group gets the same genre, action, and object assignment. You have 48 hours to write an original story from scratch.

2

Anonymous judging

Stories are stripped of author info. Judges are working editors, agents, and published authors. Top 10 of 40 in each group advance to round 2 with detailed feedback.

3

Advance & publish

Round 2 reshuffles you into new groups (~22 of 40). Top 4 advance. Final round: open genre, single pool, top 10 win cash + publication.

Publishing beyond the prize

Inkbound's commitment to advancing writers: three publishing pathways planned for the inaugural cycle.
📚

Annual print anthology

Top finishers from every challenge will be collected into a yearly print + digital anthology. Writers retain rights; Inkbound licenses one-time use. Pricing and distribution partners TBD.

🌐

Online lit journal

Finalists and honorable mentions will be featured on Inkbound Quarterly — a forthcoming online literary magazine. Paid byline. Launches with the inaugural cycle.

🤝

Editor & agent intros

Round-3 finalists will be introduced to literary agents and acquisitions editors who commit to reading challenge winners. Partnerships being established.

Charity commitment

2% of every entry fee → writer-chosen charities

At checkout, each writer will pick one of seven supported organizations. Quarterly receipts published publicly. Partner organizations TBD.
